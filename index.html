About me

Artist

Majda Chafi is an Italian visual artist and illustrator based in Paris.
She sees art as an act of self-reflection in which she tries to understand feelings, dreams, traumas, and desires.

Her works depict a subjective world, born from her imagination and a constant desire to challenge herself using different techniques.
